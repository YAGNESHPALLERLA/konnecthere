## ⭐ RECOMMENDATION: Option 2 - Modern Grey

**Why this palette:**
1. **Professional & Corporate:** Similar to LinkedIn and Indeed - trusted by job seekers
2. **High Contrast:** Excellent readability (WCAG AA+ compliant)
3. **Modern SaaS Feel:** Indigo-violet accent is contemporary and professional
4. **Versatile:** Works for all user types (candidates, HR, admin)
5. **Accessible:** All text meets contrast requirements
6. **Subtle but Distinct:** Not too bold, maintains elegance

**Implementation:** This palette will be applied across the entire site.
